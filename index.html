About Our Services

Expert AI Solutions for Modern Software

Data Sistah provides artificial intelligence (AI) development and consulting services centered on how AI is designed, developed, and applied within software-based products. Work includes supporting AI-powered applications and data science solutions intended for use within software or SaaS-style environments.

Projects may range from early-stage concepts and prototypes to more developed AI-driven applications.

Services

AI Development

Data Sistah supports the design and development of AI-powered software applications. This includes:

  • AI feature development for software applications
  • Data science solutions built for software-based platforms
  • Enhancements and improvements to existing AI implementations
  • Development support for AI-powered tools and applications

Services may support applications intended for SaaS or software-based delivery.

Large Language Model Integration

Services include working with large language models (LLMs) as part of AI-powered applications. This work may involve:

  • Experimenting with LLM behavior within applications
  • Refining how LLMs are used to support application features
  • Supporting decisions related to LLM integration and usage
  • Assisting with early-stage development involving LLM-based functionality

AI Consulting

Data Sistah provides consulting services in the field of artificial intelligence technology. Consulting work includes:

  • Advising on AI and data science application design
  • Reviewing AI-powered software implementations
  • Guiding technical decisions related to AI usage
  • Supporting teams or individuals applying AI to software projects

Consulting is focused on practical application rather than theory.

Engagement Approach

Engagements are offered on a consulting and development basis. Data Sistah works with individuals, teams, and organizations seeking support with AI development, data science applications, or AI-related software projects.

Work may support projects intended for delivery through software-based platforms or SaaS-style solutions.
